Drift Hunters

Description


In the auto racing game Drift Hunters, players drive high-performance vehicles while attempting to drift through bends to get as many points as possible. The game offers configurable automobiles, realistic driving dynamics, and a selection of racetracks. Players may challenge themselves to get better at drifting in single play or compete online against other racers. By executing lengthy, elegant drifts and earning points for both speed and style, the goal is to gain the best score possible.

Controls


Arrow keys or WASD.

Creator


Ilya Kaminetsky
